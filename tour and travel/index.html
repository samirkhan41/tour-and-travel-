Popular Destinations

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal, and is considered a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic architecture

Varanasi

Experience the romance and culture of the City of Lights, from the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre Museum.

kedarnath

Kedarnath is a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand. It's known for its natural beauty and is a popular destination for trekker

Featured Hotels

The Oberoi Amarvilas

The Oberoi Amarvilas Agra, a luxurious 5-star hotel, is known for its Mughal-inspired architecture, stunning views of the Taj Mahal, and exceptional amenities, including a spa, multiple dining options, and a heated pool .

Brijrama-Palace-Hotel

Brijrama Palace, a heritage hotel in Varanasi, offers a unique experience with its Maratha-style architecture and stunning views of the Ganges, while also providing a serene retreat for travelers.

Shivalik valley resorts

"Mountain retreat" - "Shivalik Valley Resort" is situated in the foothills of the Himalayas, offering a peaceful escape amidst stunning mountain scenery.
